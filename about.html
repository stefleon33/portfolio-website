About Stephanie Leon

Hi! My name is Stephanie Leon, and I’m excited to share my journey from working in education to becoming a full-stack web developer. After spending several years teaching and working in schools, I realized my passion for problem-solving and creativity could be channeled into the world of software development.

I decided to make a career change and dove headfirst into learning web development, mastering both front-end and back-end technologies. Today, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and more. With this new skill set, I’m eager to build applications that solve real-world problems and create seamless, dynamic user experiences.

My background in education has given me a strong foundation in communication, patience, and empathy—qualities that I believe are essential in both teaching and development. I enjoy working collaboratively, explaining complex ideas in simple terms, and delivering high-quality solutions.

I’m excited about this new chapter and look forward to applying my skills to create meaningful web applications. Whether it's building a responsive interface or developing a scalable back-end, I’m always learning and ready to take on the next challenge.

Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see some of the projects I’ve worked on!
